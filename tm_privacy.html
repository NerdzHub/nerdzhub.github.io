Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 9, 2025  ·  Last updated: April 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how NerdzHub ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the True Manifest mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information you directly provide when using the App, such as:

  • Content you create within the App (e.g., manifests, notes, or lists).
  • Feedback or support requests you send to us.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain technical data to improve the App, including:

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version.
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information.
  • General usage statistics (e.g., feature interactions, session length).

This data is collected in an aggregated or anonymized form wherever possible and does not identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App.
  • Diagnose technical problems and fix bugs.
  • Respond to your support requests.
  • Understand how users interact with the App so we can enhance the experience.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Content you create in True Manifest is stored locally on your device. If cloud sync features are available, data may be stored using secure, encrypted cloud services. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services (such as Apple's frameworks or analytics providers) that collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with through the App.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data.
  • The right to opt out of certain data collection practices.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. You can delete locally stored data by uninstalling the App.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
