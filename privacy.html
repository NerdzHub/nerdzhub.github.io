Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Nerdzhub (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the HairTransplant AI mobile application (“app”).

1. Data Collection

We collect the following data when you use the app:

  • Images uploaded for analysis
  • Firebase Anonymous ID
  • Device information (e.g., IP address, operating system)
  • Payment-related details (handled by RevenueCat)

2. Data Usage

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • To generate AI-based analyses, such as graft count and evaluation scores
  • To provide services like storing results and displaying recent analyses
  • To improve the app’s functionality and user experience

3. Data Sharing

We share user data with the following third parties for specific purposes:

  • RevenueCat: For payment processing
  • Firebase: For data storage and analytics
  • OpenAI API: For AI-based analysis of uploaded images

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Firebase and Google Cloud Storage. We use encryption and access controls to protect your information.

5. Data Retention

We retain your uploaded images and analysis results until you choose to delete them. You can request the removal of your data at any time.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Update or correct your data
  • Request the deletion of your data

7. Use by Minors

The app is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. If you are under 18, please do not use the app.

8. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. If changes are made, users will be notified within the app.

9. Third-Party Links

The app does not include links to third-party websites or services.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hey@nerdzhub.com.
